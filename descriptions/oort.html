Geophysical Fluid Dynamics Laboratory (GFDL) Global Atmospheric Circulation

BRIEF

Oort (1983) gives global atmospheric circulation statistics here.

The sources of data include the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) Circulation Library, NCDC, National Meteorological Center, National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR), British Meteorological Office in Bracknell, England, and the U.S. Air Force Environmental Technical Applications Center (ETAC). A collection of 15 years of global rawinsonde data and all available surface land and surface ship reports was obtained for analyses.
