Data Library ECMF_CY41-47

ECMF CY41-47

Forecast

  • Time range: 0-46 days
  • Atmosphere Resolution: Tco639 L137 (about 16 km) up to day 15 and Tco319 (about 32 km) after day 15
  • Ens. Size: 51 (control + 50 perturbed)
  • Frequency: 2 times per week (Mon/Thu)

Reforecast

  • Type: On the fly
  • Length: past 20 years
  • Frequency: 2 times per week (Mon/Thu)
  • Size: 11 (control + 10 perturbed)
  • Ocean Resolution: 1/4 degree
  • Active sea ice: Yes

Updates

  • Dec 6, 2024: ECMF.forecast is linked to CY41-47.forecast
  • Dec 6, 2024: ECMF.reforecast is linked to CY41-47.reforecast
  • Feb 5, 2025: The original configuration of CY41-47 has been reduced for the reforecast dataset up to December 29, 2022, following the instructions from the owner. After this date, the system corresponds to CY48. If you require data beyond this date, you can find it in the CY48 dataset .
