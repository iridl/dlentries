UNAM Monthly Precipitation and Maximum and Minimum Temperature Analyses for Mexico and Surroundings

This monthly gridded precipitation data set is from the Centro de Ciencias de la Atmósfera (CCA) at the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM). 

Data source:       The analysis was based on daily station precipitation, maximum and
                   minimum temperature data from CLICOM (for Mexico area), and monthly
                   data from the Global Historical Climatology Network (GHCN) version 2
                   (for the rest of the region).  Climate Research Unit (CRU) monthly 
                   gridded precipitation and maximum and minimum temperature data sets 
                   were used as the 'first guess' for the Cressman objective analysis.

Resolution :       0.5 degree latitude by 0.5 degree longitude

Period :           From 1901 to 2002

Domain :           The analysis domain is (-140.25W - 59.75W, 4.75N - 45.25N).
                   Over Mexico, Southern U.S., Central America, Caribbean Sea and
                   Northern South America

Analysis Scheme :  Cressman (1959)

Units :            mm/month, degrees Celsius

Data set author and contact information: Matias Méndez, email: matias@atmosfera.unam.mx / Ernesto Caetano, email: caetano@servidor.unam.mx
