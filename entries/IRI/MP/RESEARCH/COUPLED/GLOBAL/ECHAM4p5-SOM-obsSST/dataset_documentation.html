ECHAM4.5 T42 AGCM with mixed layer model

This model is ECHAM4.5 T42 AGCM coupled to a spatially varying but constant in time mixed layer model. This version of the model prescribes SST in the tropical and subtropical eastern and central Pacific but predicts it elsewere.
