Data Library Bi-weekly ELR Temperature Forecasts

The IRI Bi-weekly ELR Temperature Forecasts datasets in this section may be browsed; anaysis and visualization may be performed in the Data Library and figures may be downloaded. However, downloads of data files are restricted to licensed users. More details on the license Terms & Conditions can be found here. If you wish to request permission to access downloads, browse to your dataset or variable of interest, click the Data Files or Data Tables tabs. You will be directed to a sign-up/in page; then to a form to fill and submit. Depending on your information, you will be granted permission or sent a contract, within 5 business days.
