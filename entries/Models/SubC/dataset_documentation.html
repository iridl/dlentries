The SubC (formerly SubX) model output should be referred to as the SubC data and referenced using the DOI: 10.1175/BAMS-D-18-0270.1. In addition, an acknowledgment similar to the following should be included in any publication: “We acknowledge the agencies that supported the original SubX system, and we thank the climate modeling groups (Environment Canada, NASA, NOAA/NCEP, NRL and University of Miami) for producing and making available their model output. NOAA/MAPP, ONR, NASA, NOAA/NWS jointly provided coordinating support and led development of the original system.”
