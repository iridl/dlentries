Cayan Analysis of COADS

This dataset was created by Dr. Daniel R. Cayan of the Scripps Institution of Oceanography, Climate Research Division 0224, Univeristy of California, La Jolla, CA 92093-0224. The monthly net heat flux, and the individual components of the flux where computed from COADS. More information about this dataset can be obtained in the Journal of Climate.

Reference

Cayan, D., 1992: Latent and Sensible Heat Flux Anomalies over the Northern
	Oceans: The Connection to Monthly Atmospheric Circulation. J.
	Climate, 5, 354-369.
