DESCRIPTION

Enclosed are data from CIESIN's Global subnational rates of child underweight status database. Further documentation for these data is available in the enclosed catalog and on the CIESIN Poverty Mapping web site at: http://www.ciesin.columbia.edu/povmap

This is the beta release of this product. See the Poverty Mapping home page for additional information on the product.

FILE TYPE

The data are stored in ASCII grid format. The ASCII format stores the data as simple text. The first lines of the file give the resolution, number of rows and columns, no data value, and rotation factors. The data are stored in geographic coordinates of decimal degrees based on the World Geodetic System spheroid of 1984 (WGS84).

INDIVIDUAL GRIDS

UW Percent of children, under the age of 5, who are underweight (year period: 1990-2002), 2.5 minute resolution
UWQ Percent of children, under the age of 5, who are underweight (year period: 1990-2002), 0.25-degree resolution
UWU5POP Estimate of children, under the age of 5, who are underweight, year 2000, 0.25-degree resolution
UWU5POPQ Estimate of children, under the age of 5, who are underweight, year 2000, 2.5-minute resolution
U5POP Count of children under age 5, year 2000, 2.5-minute resolution
U5POPQ Count of children under age 5, year 2000, 0.25-degree resolution
