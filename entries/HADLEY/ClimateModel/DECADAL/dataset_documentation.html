HadCM3 ENSEMBLES Stream 2

Exported from ECMWF ECFS data archive to MetO in native PP format: Converted to netCDF using BADC/MOHC Virtual Variable Suite version 0.3.0

Hindcasts

Yearly start dates: 1st November 1960 to 2005 10 year runs, except 1965, 1975, 1985, 1995, 2005 are 30 year runs 9 members (with runids z02g0 = standard, z02g[1-8] = perturbed)

DePreSys

1. Atmosphere output: monthly means (mm)
/ENSEMBLES/stream2/PPE/ z02g[0-8]/z02g[0-8]_mm_[name-code]_[start-date]-[end-date] name-code: from /ENSEMBLES/FieldCodesTable.xls column 1 start-date: in format [year][month][day][hour][min] end-date: in format [year][month][day][hour][min] e.g. z02g1/z02g1_mm_pr_196011010000-197011010000.nc contains monthly mean total precipitation rate output for a 10 year run starting from November 1960. e.g. z02g1/z02g1_mm_pr_196511010000-199511010000.nc contains monthly mean total precipitation rate output for a 30 year run starting from November 1965.

2. Ocean output: seasonal means (sm)
ENSEMBLES/stream2/PPE/ z02g[0-8]/z02g[0-8]_sm_[name-code]_[start-date]-[end-date] name-code: e.g. z02g1/z02g1_sm_so5.0_196011010000-197011010000.nc contains seasonal mean salinity output at the 5.0m ocean level for a 10 year run starting from November 1960. e.g. z02g1/z02g1_sm_so5.0_196511010000-199511010000.nc contains seasonal mean salinity output at the 5.0m ocean level for a 30 year run starting from November 1965.

Climatology

/ENSEMBLES/clim_mm/atmos/ [runid]/[runid] _mm_[name-code]_[start-date]-[end-date] runid: from runid table below name-code: e.g. aenwh/aenwh_mm_pr0_195012010000-198912010000.nc contains monthly mean total precipitation rate output from December 1950 to the end of November 1989 and provides the climatology for the standard ensemble member z02g0. e.g. aeyjc/aeyjc_mm_pr0_198912010000-204012010000.nc contains monthly mean total precipitation rate output from December 1989 to the end of November 2040 and provides the climatology for ensemble member z02g2.
