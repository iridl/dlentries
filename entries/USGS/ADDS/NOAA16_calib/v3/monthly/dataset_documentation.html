Description of monthly composited and reprojected NDVI data for Africa

Documentation on dekadal NDVI composites for Africa can be found on the following page from the USGS Africa Data Dissemination Service: http://edcsnw4.cr.usgs.gov/adds/readme.php?symbol=ne. The data available in the Data Library has been composited by month rather than dekad and reprojected onto a 0.1° x 0.1° grid over the domain 20°W to 55°E and 40°S to 40°N. The monthly composites for Africa were received from Chris Funk of the University of California, Santa Barbara.
