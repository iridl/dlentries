Princeton Meteorological Forcings for West/Central/East Africa

The website associated with this dataset is http://hydrology.princeton.edu/data.metdata_africa.php.

The following information is from the "Readme" file associated with this dataset: 


Source:         Princeton University
Name:           Princeton Meteorological Forcings for West/Central/East Africa
Period:         Jan-1979 - Dec-2005
Times:          Daily
Coverage:       Global
Resolution:     0.1-degree
Grid:           Latitude-longitude
Type:           Observational-Reanalysis hybrid
Related Sites:	http://hydrology.princeton.edu/data/index.html
Publications:   Chaney, N., and J. Sheffield, 2012: Development of a 50-yr
		high-resolution global dataset of meteorological forcings for land surface
		modeling, J. Climate, to be submitted; 
		Sheffield, J., G. Goteti, and E. F. Wood, Development of a
		50-yr high-resolution global dataset of meteorological forcings for land
		surface modeling, J. Climate, 19 (13), 3088-3111.
Variables:	Precipitation (prcp)
		Max air temperature at 2m above ground (tmax)
		Min air temperature at 2m above ground (tmin)
		Downward longwave at surface (dlwrf)
		Downward shortwave at surface (dswrf)
		Surface pressure (pres)
		Specific humidity (shum)
		Windspeed (wind)
Formats:        NetCDF
History:	Original data	
Volume:         334 MB/year/variable
Date Created:   Sep 21 2012
Change Log:     

2) Feb 8 2013
Variables prec, dswrf, dlwrf, pres added, and all existing variables updated
to latest version.

1) Sep 21 2012
Initial version uploaded to the web for shum, tmin, tmax, wind
