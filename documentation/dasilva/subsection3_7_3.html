Oceanic friction velocity

Although the wind stress is a measurement of the force of the wind upon the ocean surface, the ocean's response (e.g. mixed layer depth) is related to the work done on the ocean, usually parameterized in terms of the cube of the atmospheric friction velocity (Hellerman and Rosenstein 1983 and references therein).
